About me

As a Data Scientist with over 4 years of industry and research experience, I'm passionate about applying my expertise to solve real-world complex challenges. Currently, I'm leading a research project analyzing narrative shifts, user engagement, and information warfare tactics on Telegram during the Russia-Ukraine conflict. This project, a collaboration with esteemed professors including Dr. Ugur Kursuncu (Georgia State University), Dr. Dilshod Achilov (UMass Dartmouth), and Dr. Valerie L. Shalin (Wright State University), is targeted for publication in Nature Communications by March 2024 (check details here).

Before that, I worked on MLEcoFi project (A Machine Learning Ecosystem for Filament Detection), collaborated with Dr. Azim Ahmadzadeh from the University of Missouri-St. Louis. My contributions included developing a wavelet filter compression algorithm to optimize the size of astronomical images, facilitating more efficient filament-detection processes (check details here).

I'm pursuing my masters in Data Science and Analytics with a concentration in Big Data and Machine Learning at Georgia State University, anticipating graduation in May 2024 with a 4.0 GPA (details here), all while funded by a prestigious full tuition waiver scholarship. Prior to this, I have worked with Tata Consultancy Services for 3+ years, building end-to-end applications with machine learning models, data pipelines, APIs, and production deployments. Further, I was a Data Science intern at Truist Financial Bank (Atlanta, USA) and Airtel Payments Bank (Gurugram, India), respectively.
